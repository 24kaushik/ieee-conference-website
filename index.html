Objective

The International Conference on Innovation and Practices in Artificial Intelligence (ICIPAI-2025) aims to provide an international forum for sharing knowledge, methodologies, and applications of Artificial Intelligence. The conference seeks significant contributions across all major fields of AI and Soft Computing, encompassing diverse areas of engineering and management.

This platform is designed to facilitate collaboration and the exchange of ideas between researchers and practitioners from academia and industry, fostering innovation and development in AI. The conference program includes keynote lectures, plenary talks by renowned experts, and paper presentations in identified thematic tracks.

The event aspires to advance the frontiers of AI research and its practical applications, creating opportunities for learning, networking, and collaboration for participants worldwide.

Original research papers are invited on the following tracks and sub-themes. The list provided is not exhaustive, and contributions on other related themes are also welcome.
