Call for Papers

The International Conference on Intelligent Processing and Artificial Intelligence (ICIPAI 2025) invites original research papers from researchers, academics, and industry professionals worldwide.

Authors are encouraged to submit high-quality, original papers that have not been published or submitted for publication elsewhere.

Submission Guidelines:

  • All submissions must be in English
  • Papers must follow the publisher's formatting guidelines
  • Maximum length: 10 pages including figures and references
  • Submissions will undergo a double-blind peer review process
Submit Your Paper Download Paper Template

Publishing and Indexing

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the proceedings by Taylor and Francis with Scopus Indexing.

Selected high-quality papers will be recommended for publication in special issues of reputable international journals after further extension and revision.
